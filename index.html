My name is Colton Sullivan, I'm a junior here at miami majoring in software engineering. Here's a fun picture!

This site will show you stock information to keep you up to date on the latest trends and will include a bit of information about its creator, Me!

This system will allow you to look up stocks by their exchange, then you can choose a specific stock to look at the details and new of. After that the stocks info will be saved for later viewing which can be found on the third page "Stock History/Display".

The purpose of this assignment was to test out knowledge with PHP, polyon.io, Javascript, HTML, CSS, and other topics we have covered over the semester. I think this was definetly a final project because of its scope and, personally, its difficulty.
